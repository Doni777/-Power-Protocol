Power Protocol is the unified economic and infrastructure layer for blockchain entertainment. It provides a modular, programmable engagement and reward routing system that any game, app, or entertainment product can integrate. Its first application is Fableborne, a mobile action-strategy game by Pixion Games with 380,000+ beta players.
$POWER is the native token of the Power ecosystem. It's used for staking, governance, protocol usage fees, in-app reward programs, and seasonal competition systems. It acts as the coordination layer connecting multiple applications to the same economic infrastructure without binding them to a single product. Max supply: 1 billion POWER.
Fableborne is a mobile action-strategy game developed by Pixion Games and the first live application in the Power ecosystem. It achieved 380,000+ players, 108,000 peak daily active users, and $1.1M in revenue during its open beta — demonstrating genuine product-market fit for Power Protocol's economic model before the token launched.
Vesting extends 36–48 months from the December 2025 launch. Community rewards (37.2%) unlock over 48 months. Ecosystem funds (21%) over 36 months. Investor tokens (16.15%) begin releasing in 2026. 72.8% of total supply remains locked, which represents both future dilution risk and upside if ecosystem growth outpaces supply expansion.
